Frequently Asked Questions

What is SafeLinkPro?

SafeLinkPro creates secure, timed links that you can share with anyone.

How do I use it?

Enter a URL, choose a timer duration, generate a SafeLink, and share it.

Is it free?

Yes, SafeLinkPro is completely free to use.

Can I set timer durations?

Yes, choose from 15, 30, 45, or 60 seconds.

Is it secure?

SafeLinkPro uses HTTPS and encoded URLs with no data storage.
